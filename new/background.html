W H A T  W E  D O

General pest control meant for all sort of businesses, warehouses, production plant, restaurants, banks, hotels, household, offices etc. It’s a control against nuisance pest such as cockroaches, ants, termites, mosquitoes, rats and others. For some business it is a standard requirement for operational and licensing where hygiene, cleanliness and food contamination is an issue and is a stringent requirement for all food production and some ISO companies.

Pest control operator like us, will have a signed contract (normally a year) servicing the client by applying chemical and non chemical measures. We will have a schedule time agreed by both party for effective treatment exercise.
